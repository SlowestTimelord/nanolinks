Exchanges

NanoLinks is not affiliated with and cannot endorse any of the exchanges listed here.

Traditional centralized exchanges (CEXs) often require sign-up and depositing of fiat or crypto coins into a custodial wallet to use the exchange services.

Swap services are a simple way to acquire XNO but normally a bit worse exchange rate than a large exchange and often includes fees. Use with common sense and caution.
